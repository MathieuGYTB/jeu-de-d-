Rules :
1 : Roll the dice. If you obtain a number between 2 and 6,
you obtain this number in the current score.

2 : Next, choose if you roll the dice again or if you click
on hold to save the score in the total score and pass your
turn to the player 2. If you roll the dice again, you can obtain
more points but if you obtain 1, you loose every points in the
current score and you pass your turn to the player 2.

3 : The first player who obtain 100 points in total score win.

Good game !
